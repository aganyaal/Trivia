The Tales of Jomcsid from Fsjorm

This story is based on Jomcsid and all the decisions he takes to stay alive. Do come with an open mind as the misadventures and turmoil begin to unveil themselves.

WARNING: THIS GAME IS NOT TIME-BOUND!

The story opens in the village of Fsjorm with you as Jomcsid who is visited by the magician (with no name) who hands you a magical card to go and cash in for wealth beyond your wildest dreams. So you sell your all your worldly possesions and it amounts to 1000 silver coins. Which is much but also not so much bro(or female "bro". We're all about equality!). Sorry! But now you are faced with two decisions:

Lazy as you are, you decided to take an express carriage to Hellbig and miss all the adventure of walking. Well the jokes on you .The journey starts fairly well and you already visualise all the money you are going to get in Hellbig after selling your card. That is until THE TRANSPORTER decided that he did not need his passengers anymore. You are bundled out of the carriage with no money, weapons or food.Seeing as you are a useless person..............YOU DIE


The white box on your right part of the screen is showing you how much you have left.

So you decide to walk.....hmmmm, good choice.
As you are walking along the pathway, you meet an injured man.His name is Lucy(apparently his elder brother was called Luke and his mother was not of the creative kind, so he got the name Lucy )

Anyway, Lucy needs you to part with 150 silver coins out of your own goodness for his healing potion. Will you:

Lucy: Cool story bro!.I'm out!

Lucy leaves without a trace and you are now less rich and faced by two situations. Hunger or Thirst

YOU DECIDE!

As the selfish bastard that you are... he left disappointed.

But now... you are faced by two extremes and can only satisfy one...Hunger or Thirst

YOU DECIDE!

FUN FACT:

People can survive at least 40 days without food but only go less than 1 WEEK! without water. So.... Get your priorities right my friend! and start again.
